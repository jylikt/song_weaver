Title: Iron and Ash
Style: Classical / Orchestral / Cinematic
Duration: 60s
Prompt: Epic orchestral battle anthem, full strings and brass, cinematic war scene, themes of courage and sacrifice

---

[Verse 1 — strings, low brass]
The ravens circle where the banners fell
The valley fills with iron and with ash
A thousand hearts that rang like battle bells
Now still beneath the drumbeat's final crash

[Rising Pre-Chorus — strings build]
We knew what stood before us when we rode
We chose the oath above the open road

[Chorus — full orchestra]
Through iron and ash we rise
Though the world turns grey before our eyes
Every blow we strike echoes through time
We will not kneel — the future will be ours or mine

Through iron and ash we fall
If we must, we fall so others stand tall
Let the ages say we burned without shame
Through iron and ash — remember our name

[Verse 2 — woodwinds, quieter]
The generals speak in maps and distances
The soldiers speak in mud and shallow graves
But in the moment when the courage slips
It is the song that holds the broken brave

[Rising Pre-Chorus]
We knew what stood before us when we rode
We chose the oath above the open road

[Chorus — fortissimo]
Through iron and ash we rise
Though the world turns grey before our eyes
Every blow we strike echoes through time
We will not kneel — the future will be ours or mine

[Bridge — solo cello, then choir enters]
Hold the line
Hold the line
Let the fire find us standing still
Hold the line
Hold the line
Not from fear — but because we will

[Final Chorus — choir + full orchestra]
Through iron and ash we live
Every breath a gift the dead could give
Let our story be a lantern in the dark
Through iron and ash — we carry every spark
